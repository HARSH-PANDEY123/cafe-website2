In those days, the organization was a solitary store in Seattle’s memorable Pike Place Market. From only a tight customer-facing facade, ‘CAFE’ offered a portion of the world’s best crisp-cooked entire bean espressos. The name, motivated by Moby Dick, evoked the sentiment of the high oceans and the marine convention of the early espresso brokers.
